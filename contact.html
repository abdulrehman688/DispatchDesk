FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?

+

We guarantee a response within 2 hours during business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling us directly on +44 123 456 7890 where our team is available 24/7.

Is there a minimum contract length?

+

No. We offer flexible arrangements tailored to your business. We believe our service quality speaks for itself, so we don’t lock clients into long-term contracts.

Do you offer a free consultation?

+

Absolutely. Every new client starts with a free, no-obligation consultation where we assess your needs and recommend the services that will make the biggest difference.

Which dispatch platforms do you support?

+

We are certified across Autocab, iCabbi, Cab 9, Cordic, Cab Treasure and Data Master the six leading platforms in the industry. If you use a different system, contact us to discuss.

Can I use multiple services together?

+

Yes and many of our clients do. We offer bundled packages for businesses that need dispatch management alongside customer support, digital marketing or web development.

How is pricing structured?

+

Pricing is tailored to your volume and requirements. We don’t believe in one-size-fits-all pricing. Contact us and we’ll prepare a custom proposal within 24 hours.
